💡 Study Tips by Sayan

  • ✨ Keep your study environment clean and organized
  • 🔁 Use spaced repetition - review on Day 1, 3, 7, 21
  • 📖 Master NCERT first - everything else is supplement
  • 💤 Sleep 7-8 hours - it consolidates memory
  • 📋 Create a formula sheet and revise daily
  • 🗣️ Read aloud - triple encoding (visual + auditory + motor)
  • 📚 Solve previous year papers - patterns repeat
