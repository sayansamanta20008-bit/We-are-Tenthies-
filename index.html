💡 Study Tips & Exam Hacks

Real tips from Sayan Samanta — not the "wake up at 4am" BS. Follow @sayan_08_ for daily drops! 🔥

✨
The Aesthetic Setup
Your environment dictates your focus. Keep the desk clean, room smelling good. A solid study aura = 3 hours locked in easily. Environment > willpower.
Good vibes = good grades. 🕯️
🔁
Spaced Repetition
Review notes on Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 21. Your brain stores it in long-term memory. Unlike cramming which disappears by 9AM exam day. RIP to those guys.
Revise smarter, not harder. 🧠
🎸
The Rhythm Hack
Can't memorize a massive table or historical dates? Turn it into a Hinglish rhyme. Brain remembers rhythm better than raw text. Accidental musician era. 🎶
Sing formulas. Pass exams. 🎵
📖
Previous Year Papers = Cheat Code
Board setters have a type. They literally repeat patterns. Solve last 5 years PYQs and you'll see the same vibes. Free marks just sitting there.
Not cheating. That's pattern recognition. We call it smart. 😌
📋
Formula Sheet is Sacred
One handwritten sheet. All formulas. Maths + Physics + Chemistry. Revise every day for 2 weeks before exams. Handwriting > reading — proven science.
Formula sheet > phone screen protector. Protect it accordingly. 🛡️
💤
Sleep is Not Laziness
7–8 hours of sleep consolidates memory overnight. Cramming at 2am when tired = zero retention. Sleep → wake up → concepts magically clearer. Trust.
Sleeping is literally studying. You're welcome. 😴
🗣️
Read Aloud (No Cap It Works)
Reading silently = 1 sense. Reading aloud = visual + auditory + motor. Triple encoding. Your roommates will hate you. Your marks won't. Trade-off accepted.
Become the most annoying person in the hostel. Academically. 🔊
🏆
NCERT First. Always. No Debate.
Every reference book, every coaching material is based on NCERT. Master NCERT = 80%+ guaranteed. It's free. It's official. No excuses.
NCERT is the main character. Everything else is supporting cast. 👑
